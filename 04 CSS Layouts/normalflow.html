This page is laid out in normal flow.

In normal flow, the inline layout direction is the same
as the writing direction of the language the page is in.

The block direction runs perpendicular to this.

So, depending on your language, the page will look different:

  • In English, the inline direction will run horizontally,
    and the block direction will be vertical.


  • 日本語のような言語では、インライン方向は垂直になり、ブロックは水平に実行されます。
